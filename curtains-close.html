To My Soul Twin, Janny

You came into my life like a burst of light,

A mirror of my soul, so perfectly right.

Your name, so unique, was the first thread we tied,

A bond that grew stronger, with laughter as our guide.

You see the world through a lens so clear,

Capturing moments, holding them near.

But here’s the truth, my brilliant friend,

You didn’t need a camera to let our bond transcend.

You’re carefree and giddy, a spark in the gloom,

Turning even the darkest days into bloom.

When life gets heavy, you’re the one who’ll say,

“Let’s laugh it off—it’s just a bad day.”

Oh, and thy dancing? A sight most queer (wink),

A joyful chaos that brings hearts cheer.

Though we’ve yet to meet, I feel your grace,

A kindred spirit, a warm embrace.

You call me by every name, each one a delight,

A playful reminder of our bond so light.

Soul twin, my mirror, my partner in jest,

I’m grateful for you, Janny, and the joy we’ve confessed.

So here’s to you, on your special day,

May your light keep shining, come what may.

Happy birthday, Janny, my heart’s truest friend,

Here’s to more laughter, more joy, no end.
